The Philosophy

What is Homeopathy?

Homeopathy is a gentle, holistic system of medicine that has been used for over 200 years. Rather than simply treating a named condition or suppressing symptoms, it seeks to understand and stimulate the body's own natural healing intelligence.

Founded on the principle of 'like cures like', homeopathic remedies — prepared from plants, minerals and natural substances — are highly individualised to each patient's unique constellation of symptoms, personality, and life experience.

  • Treats the whole person — body, mind and emotions — not just the disease
  • Suitable for all ages, from newborns to the elderly
  • Natural, non-toxic and safe alongside conventional medicine
  • Seeks the root cause rather than managing surface symptoms
  • Deeply individual — every person receives their own unique remedy

About Louise

A Homeopath with Heart

I'm Louise MacDonald — a practical homeopath with over 30 years of experience walking alongside patients through some of life's most challenging chapters. My journey into homeopathy began long before my formal training, born of a deep conviction that the body holds extraordinary wisdom when given the right support.

In 2005 I graduated as a Licentiate of the College of Practical Homeopathy (LCPH) — a rigorous, clinically-focused training that prepared me to work with the full complexity of human health. In the years since, I have worked with thousands of patients, from newborn babies to grandparents, supporting everything from acute infections to decades-long chronic conditions.

Outside the consultation room, I am a wife and proud mother of two, and a delighted grandmother to a young grandson who brings immeasurable joy. Family is everything to me — and it shapes how I practise. I bring the same warmth, patience and genuine care to every patient that I would want for my own loved ones.

My consultations are unhurried, compassionate and deeply listening. I believe that to find the right remedy, you must first truly know the person.
